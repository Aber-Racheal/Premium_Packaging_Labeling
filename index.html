Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common questions about our packaging, ordering process, and deliveries.

Do you offer deliveries, and which areas do you cover?

Yes, we offer deliveries within town and to selected surrounding areas. For customers outside our immediate location, we can arrange delivery through trusted transport services.

How much does delivery cost?

Delivery costs depend on your location and order size. We always confirm and agree on the delivery fee with you before dispatching your order.

What are your working days and hours?

We operate from Monday to Saturday during standard business hours. Orders and inquiries received outside working hours are attended to the next business day.

Do you handle custom packaging and labeling?

Absolutely. We specialize in customized packaging and labeling tailored to your product, brand colors, and quantity requirements.

Is there a minimum order quantity?

Minimum order quantities vary depending on the type of packaging. We are happy to advise based on your needs and budget.

How do payments work?

Payments are agreed upon before production begins. This helps us ensure transparency, proper planning, and timely delivery of your order.
